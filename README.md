## Why This Matters Now

Three forces are driving sovereign and on-premises AI demand in 2026:

1. **Regulation** — The EU AI Act and equivalent laws require high-risk organizations to maintain direct control over their models and data. Cloud APIs make that legally difficult.
2. **Data residency** — Enterprises in finance, healthcare, defense, and government are legally prohibited from sending sensitive data to third-party cloud APIs. On-premises is their only path to GenAI.
3. **Sovereignty** — Markets including Germany, Saudi Arabia, India, and Pakistan require citizen and state data to remain within national borders. Sovereign AI infrastructure is the only compliant option.

Most AI PMs have never operated inside these constraints. I have shipped there for over six years.
